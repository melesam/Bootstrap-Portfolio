About Mel

Mel is a powerful superhero who has adopted the secret identity of a hardworking corporate professional to become closer to humanity. This warrior princess possesses power by the Gods to fight against evil in all of its forms. When not consumed with maintaining her secret identity, she fights crime and acts as a positive role model for all women.
